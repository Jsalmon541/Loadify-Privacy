# Privacy Policy for Loadify

**Last updated: JUNE 27, 2026**

Loadify ("the app," "we," "us") is a personal productivity app. This policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: everything you create stays on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your data with anyone.

## Data we collect

**None.** Loadify has no servers, no user accounts, and no network connection for your content. We do not collect personal information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, location, or any other data, and we have no ability to see, access, or receive anything you enter into the app.

## What the app stores, and where

Everything you create in the app — your tasks, folders, notes, habits, completion history, and settings — is stored **only on your device**, in the app's private storage sandbox. This data is never uploaded, transmitted, or backed up to us or to any third party by the app. It remains entirely under your control. Specifically:

- Your main data store is encrypted at rest on your device using strong, industry-standard encryption (currently AES-GCM). The encryption key is stored separately in your device's secure system storage (such as the iOS Keychain or Android Keystore) and **never leaves your device**.
- To display the **home-screen widget**, a small amount of information is stored on your device in an **unencrypted** form so the widget can read it while your device is locked. This information **never leaves your device** and is never shared with us or any third party. See the next section for exactly what this includes.

## Home-screen widget

If you use the app's home-screen widget, the app shares the limited information the widget needs to display with its own widget component. Depending on which widgets you use, this can include your **task titles, priority levels, and progress**; the **name and timing of an active focus session**; and the **names and scores of habits you track**. It does **not** include the notes or details inside your tasks, which stay encrypted.

This sharing happens **entirely on your device**, through a local data-sharing mechanism provided by your operating system (such as Apple's App Groups) that lets an app and its own widget share data locally. This information is **not** sent off the device and is **not** shared with any external service or third party.

## Backups you create

The app lets you optionally export a backup of your data. When you do this, the backup is **encrypted with a password that you choose** and saved to a location **you select** (for example, your Files app or a cloud drive).

- The app does not send this backup anywhere on its own — you decide where it goes.
- If you choose to save your backup to a cloud service (such as iCloud Drive), that file is then handled according to **that provider's** privacy policy, not ours.
- Keep your backup password safe: because the backup is encrypted, **we cannot recover or reset it for you**, and a lost password means the backup cannot be opened.

## Sharing a task with someone else

The app lets you optionally send an individual task to another person. When you do this, the task is **encrypted with a password** (one you choose or one the app generates for you) into a file that you then share through whatever channel you pick — for example, Messages, email, or AirDrop — or save to your device.

- The app does not send this file anywhere on its own — you decide who receives it and how.
- The password is shared at your discretion; for your security we recommend sending it through a **different channel** than the file itself. As a convenience, the app can copy the password to your device clipboard when you tap to copy it.
- As with backups, **we cannot recover the password for you**, and the recipient needs it to open the shared task.
- Once the file leaves the app, it is handled according to the privacy policy of whatever service or app you use to send or store it, not ours.

## Device permissions

Loadify does **not** request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, calendar, or reminders, and does not track you across other apps or websites.

## Third-party services

Loadify does **not** include any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking software. No third-party SDK in the app collects or receives your data.

## How to delete your data

Because all of your data is stored locally on your device, you can permanently delete all of it at any time by deleting the app from your device. Removing the app erases its local encrypted storage. There is no separate account or server-side copy for us to delete, because none exists.
(Note: If you utilize full-device backups, such as Apple's iCloud Backup, an encrypted copy of your app data may be stored in your device backup. You can manage this in your device's system settings).

## Children's privacy

Loadify is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. (Because the app collects no information from anyone, this is true for users of all ages.)

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
